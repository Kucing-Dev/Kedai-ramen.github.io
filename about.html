🍜 Tentang MiawChan Ramen

Didirikan dengan semangat dan rasa, Ramen MiawChan menghadirkan cita rasa Jepang dalam setiap seruputannya.

Our Story

It started in a small kitchen in Fukuoka, where our founder experimented with traditional broth techniques and secret spices. Flavit Ramen was born out of love for authenticity, quality, and ramen artistry.

Our Philosophy

We believe ramen is not just food – it’s an experience. From the texture of the noodles to the umami-rich broth, every bowl we serve is made to warm your soul.

Why Choose Flavit?

  • 🍥 Premium ingredients from Japan
  • 👨‍🍳 Crafted by ramen chefs with decades of experience
  • 🌏 Sustainability & local partnership values
